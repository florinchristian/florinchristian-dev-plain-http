About me

Hello there! My name is Florin Cristi Gheorghe. I am a 19 years old student and programming enthusiast from Craiova, Romania.

Since I was 10 years old I was passionate about computers and technology, not only playing computer games but also knowing how to solve computers' software problems and even creating my own apps and games.
